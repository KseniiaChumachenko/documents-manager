# Ideation
In case we can make AI-like features for free—let's introduce a couple of them. 
1. Generation of the document based on prompt. 
2. Suggestions of the values in the document.
3. Business analysis. Documents are produced by a business, and it's important to understand how the business is doing. Based on the numbers, can we create charts, etc.

Where it's coming from: Sometimes there is an end number that a customer has to be charged, and values of what components will cost have to be tuned for that end number. So the prompt could be "Generate invoice for company Antareks for air-conditioning system for 2000UAH". Based on this prompt invoice could be generated as a suggestion and accepted or edited by the end user.
