# 04 — Військовий збір (Military Levy) & ЄСВ (Unified Social Contribution)

*Current as of June 2026. Two SEPARATE charges. Both distinct from ПДФО (18%) — see `05-payroll-employees.md`. 2026 МЗП = 8 647 грн.*

## Військовий збір (military levy)

**Legal basis:** ПКУ, підрозділ 10 розділу XX «Перехідні положення», **п. 16-1**. Temporary tax, levied until the Verkhovna Rada ends it.

### Standard rate on salaries/personal income — 5%

- **Historic:** 1.5% (2014 → Nov 2024).
- **Raised to 5%** by **Закон № 4015-IX від 10.10.2024**, **effective 01.12.2024** (published 30.11.2024). **2026 rate: 5%.**
- **Base:** salary, civil-law contract income, passive income (deposit interest, rent, royalties), winnings — same base as ПДФО (gross accrued income). Employers withhold as tax agents (ст. 168).
- **Transitional rule:** income accrued for periods up to and incl. **November 2024** stays at **1.5%** regardless of payout date.
- **Exception:** pay of військовослужбовці / certain security personnel remains at **1.5%**.

### Військовий збір on єдинники (single-tax payers) — NEW from 2025

Did not exist before 2025; created by **Закон № 4015-IX**, codified in підрозд. 10 розд. XX п. 16-1, **effective 01.01.2025**, continuing in 2026.

| Group | 2026 rate / base | 2026 amount | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Group 3** (ФОП + ЮО) | **1% of income** | variable | **Quarterly** (with the ЄП declaration, within 50 days of quarter-end) |
| **Groups 1, 2, 4** (ФОП) | **10% of МЗП** (1 Jan) | **864,70 грн/month** (8 647 × 10%) | **Monthly**, by the 20th (may prepay up to a year) |

- **Mobilized ФОП:** exempt from military levy (and ЄП/reporting) for the whole period of military service, on submission of documents.

## ЄСВ — Єдиний соціальний внесок

**Legal basis:** **Закон № 2464-VI від 08.07.2010.** A **social-insurance contribution, not a tax**; funds pension/social insurance.

### Rate — 22%

- **22%** of the base (ст. 8). Stable since 2016; **unchanged for 2026.**

### Base, cap, minimum (employees) — 2026

- **Base for employees:** gross accrued salary; paid **by the employer**, on top of gross (not withheld).
- **Minimum monthly ЄСВ:** base ≥ minimum wage → **8 647 × 22% = 1 902,34 грн** (2026).
- **Maximum base:** **20 × МЗП = 172 940 грн/month** → max monthly ЄСВ **38 046,80 грн**.
- **Disabled employees (employer):** reduced **8.41%** (specialised disabled-persons' enterprises: 5.3% / 5.5%). Applies to employers paying for disabled employees, not to ФОП "за себе".
- ⚠ For servicemembers/police the max base is 15 × МЗП — secondary-source figure; verify against Закон 2464 if load-bearing.

### ЄСВ "за себе" for ФОП

- **Minimum monthly (2026): 1 902,34 грн** (22% of МЗП), regardless of income earned. Єдинники may pay quarterly.
- **Wartime status — IMPORTANT for 2026:** the blanket "voluntary ЄСВ" relief that єдинники had early in martial law was **abolished from 01.01.2025**. From 2025 onward ЄСВ "за себе" is **mandatory**, and **remains mandatory in 2026**.
- **Still-valid exemptions (2026):** ФОП who are **age pensioners** or **persons with disabilities**; **mobilized** ФОП (period of service); ФОП **simultaneously employed** where the employer already pays ЄСВ ≥ minimum. Skipping ЄСВ means those months don't count toward страховий стаж.

## How they combine on a salary

On a typical salary three separate items apply: **ПДФО 18%** + **військовий збір 5%** (both withheld from employee) + **ЄСВ 22%** (paid on top by employer). Worked example in `05-payroll-employees.md`.
